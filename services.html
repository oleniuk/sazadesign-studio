Services

Saza Design Studio specializes on creating the author's interior design for both private and public spaces, with maximum attention to detail and the wishes of the customer. Our team takes over everything from developing a design project to the maintenance of construction work and object assembly.

We also offer commercial realistic visualizations of architecture and interior spaces, product visualization for Developers, Real Estates, Architects and Manufacturers. With our knowledge in concept design we will turn your technical plans into a unique artistic visual iteration.

Interior design

The interior design project is a comprehensive solution for organizing space, consisting of the development of planning solutions, the concept of the future interior, 3D visualization, the specification of all finishing materials and furniture, as well as a full set of working drawings for its subsequent implementation.

As a result, you will receive detailed instructions for self-implementation.

Implementation

1. Designer's control
Author's supervision is a set of works aimed at the author's control over the execution of the planned design project. Each project is individual, so the implementation may have its own difficulties and peculiarities. We will help you with this, control the process, make changes to the project if necessary, advise all third-party contractors and builders, and solve the nuances that have appeared.

2. Complete set
Complete set is organization of a timely procurement process for all items according to the estimate, from the request for a quotation (RF). preparation and approval of samples of materials to be painted with the Customer, verification of articles, delivery and sale of commercial items. With this service, we take care of everything down to the last detail in the decor, working out the design of the project thoroughly.

Decoration / Homestyling

Interior decoration is the final puzzle in the work on the space. We offer services for decorating both the interiors we have implemented and any other premises that require elaboration to the smallest detail. We will select original interior items, furniture, textiles and decor to complement the space and achieve maximum authenticity and comfort.

Or maybe do you feel that something is missing in your house and you need to freshen up the interior? It is not at all necessary to resort to large-scale redevelopment in order to transform the interior. Often simple changes can dramatically change a space, and it will sparkle with new colors! We'll create a collage, make a list of small proposed changes, and guide you with the choice of color palette, lighting, furniture, finishing materials, and accessories.

Visualization of 3D projects

Are you Developer, Real Estate or Manufacturer and you need professional marketing materials to sell your properties or present your product in the best way to your customers?

We can help you in it, to translate your design vision into a realistic visualization. We know how to create the perfect presentation of a project with a maximum attention to details.

We do 3D visualizations of any complexity - from interior elements to territories with a comprehensive development.

Our range of services includes:
